# Gherkin & Caper Burger

## Ingredients
- 8.7kg Beef Mince
- 400g capers
- 225g gherkings
- 6egg yolks
- 2tbsp english mustard
- 4 tbsp shallots
- 8 Shallots

## Method
1. finely chop the capers, gherkins and shallots
2. add everything to a stand mixer and mix till well distributed and even
    - If no stand mixer mix with hands
3. form into patties
4. cook as desired
