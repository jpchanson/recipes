# American Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 230g self raising flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 4 large eggs (separated)
- 250ml milk
- pinch of salt

## Method
1. seive flour and baking powder into a bowl
2. make a well in the middle of the flour
3. beat milk and yolks together and place in the flour well
4. mix to a smooth batter (a consistency like double cream)
5. whisk egg whites to stiff peaks with salt
6. fold whites into batter
7. cook on a medium/high heat till bubbles form
8. when done reserve in a wam place while you cook the rest of the batch
