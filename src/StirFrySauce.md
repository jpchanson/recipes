# StirFry Sauce

## Ingredients
- 120ml chicken stock
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice wine
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp sesame oil

## Method
1. combine all ingredients in a small pot
2. bring to the boil over a high heat
3. reduce by 1/4
4. take off heat and use in stir fry
