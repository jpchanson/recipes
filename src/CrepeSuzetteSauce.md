# Crepe Suzette Sauce

## Ingredients
- 150ml orange juice (2 large oranges)
- juice of half a lemon
- zest of 1 orange
- 150g butter
- 100ml orange liquor (grand marnier preffered)
- 60g sugar

## Method
1. Melt butter
2. add juice, jext and sugar
3. bring to a boil
4. simmer for 5 mins
5. before serving add orange liquor and flambe at the table.
